About

I'm a geospatial data analyst with 4+ years of experience across Southeast Asia and the United States, translating spatial data into decision-ready insights and tools through research, consulting, pre-sales solutioning, and analytics roles.

At GoTo Group (Gojek), I design and analyze experiments to evaluate routing and cartography improvements, building self-serve data products for engineering and product teams. As co-founder of Datakota, I lead geospatial intelligence consulting across Indonesia — from retail market analysis to climate vulnerability mapping.

My edge is working across the technical and the strategic — I speak both GIS and the boardroom, and I've done it from Jakarta to New York City.

Experienced across urban science, climate, mobility, FMCG, healthcare, and real estate use cases, with a strong track record of cross-functional collaboration aligning analysis with stakeholder needs.
